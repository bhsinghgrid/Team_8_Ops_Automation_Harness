Core Problem

The Chaos of Production Search & Merchandising

Competing Rules

Merchandisers boost products on synonyms while campaigns suppress them, causing rules to "fight" over search scope.

Ghost Boosts

Out-of-stock items boosted in active slots, wasting prime screen real estate with empty zero-result slots.

Blind Releases

Unvalidated catalog ingestions and index updates deployed directly, triggering CTR degradation or silent latency spikes.
